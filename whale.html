Whale

Meet the whale! Whales are large marine mammals found in all oceans throughout the world. Their diets are based on their species type: baleen whale filter-feeding on other smaller marine mammals like krill and fish while while toothed whale feed on wider range like fish, seal and squaid. Whales plays a very vital role in the ocean, not only redistributing nutrients but also maintaining the food chain balance.
